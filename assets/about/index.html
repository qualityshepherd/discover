feed about

discover is a curated list of RSS feeds worth following. Feeds from people writing, making and thinking. High quality signal.

Browse: playlists are curated collections of RSS feeds that share a connection. Browse them, read posts inline, find things worth following.

Follow: click +follow on any source to add it to your Feed. Your Feed is your personal timeline. All recent posts from sources you follow, newest first. It lives in your browser, no account needed.

Feed: import an OPML file from another reader. Export to take your feed elsewhere. Add your favorite RSS feeds outside of discover by URL. Curate your own playlist.

Mentions: if your site is listed in discover, you can follow your ↩ mentions feed to see when other writers in the directory link to your work.
